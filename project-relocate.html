Moving from sketches to polish meant dialing in the look, smoothing the flows, and making sure every core feature pulled its weight.

Accessible contrast. Every combination now meets WCAG AA so the interface stays inclusive.

Consistent language. Aligned spacing and iconography to reduce relearning between flows.

Friction-free flows. Simplified interactions we saw in walkthroughs to keep momentum high.

Feature essentials

Integrated discovery. Filters, search, and location services work together to surface the right facility fast.

Calm management. Notifications and schedules keep every booking, alert, and update in one hub.

Informed decisions. Facility pages surface amenities, reviews, and distance so choices feel confident.

Usability sessions called out three fixes we shipped: make the map/list toggle unmistakable, label the bookings view as "Your Bookings," and give filters prime placement so people always know how to narrow results.
