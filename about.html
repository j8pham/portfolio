my design principles

i've come up with my own design principles that guide me every day. these are the ones i care about the most.

be considerate

show care for every person who uses your products. recognize exclusions and create inclusive experiences.

build trust

be genuine and transparent. show users and stakeholders that you act in their best interests.

create consistency

use familiar patterns to make experiences recognizable. help your teams achieve goals with less effort.
